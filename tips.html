Tips to Reduce Water Footprint

Turn off Faucets While Brushing

Save water by turning off the faucet while brushing your teeth.

Fix Leaky Faucets

Repair any leaky faucets to prevent water wastage.

Collect Rainwater for Plants

Use collected rainwater to water your plants instead of tap water.

Take Shorter Showers

Reduce water usage by taking shorter showers.

Use a Dishwasher Efficiently

Make sure your dishwasher is fully loaded before running to save water.

Install Low-Flow Showerheads

Replace traditional showerheads with low-flow alternatives to conserve water.

Reuse Cooking Water

Reuse water from boiling vegetables to water houseplants.
